Bee Polination

Did you know that about one third of the fruits and vegetables we eat are dependent on bees for polination?


Apples, cranberries, melons, broccoli, blueberries and cherries are 90-percent dependent on bee pollination. Almonds, depend entirely on bees for pollination.
